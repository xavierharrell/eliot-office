Eliot believes in a consultative approach and maintains personalized, one on one relationships with all its merchants, providing them with on-demand support and 24/7 customer service. To provide this level of support, Eliot maintains a network of local offices in cities across the nation. More than a voice on the phone, Eliot representatives supply personalized assistance and on-going customer service.
